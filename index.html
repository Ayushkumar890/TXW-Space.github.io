About

What are we trying to solve?


As the possibility of space tourism to Mars becomes increasingly tangible, there is a requirement for an application that streamlines and enriches the journey for passengers.



What solution are we trying to build?


An app to assist space travelers in every aspect of their journey, from organizing travel arrangements and reserving tickets and accommodations to scheduling training sessions and securing spots for Martian exploration while remaining user-friendly, accessible, convenient, captivating, and emphasizing safety.
